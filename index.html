Features

Everything a triathlete needs

From building your first training block to analyzing the splits on your last interval session.

📅

Personalized Training Plans

Generates a fully periodized training plan based on your goal race, fitness level, available equipment, and weekly hours. Adapts as you progress.

⚡

Training Zones

Automatic calculation of heart rate, pace, and power zones from your PBs or real activity data. Run zones (VDOT), bike zones (FTP), swim zones (CSS).

🔄

Strava Sync

Connect your Strava account to automatically import completed workouts. GEMS reads your activity data and matches it against your training plan.

📊

Performance Analysis

Per-activity metrics: average pace, power, heart rate, TSS estimate, VDOT, W/kg. Lap-by-lap split tables for intervals and long runs.

🏥

Injury Adaptation

Declare an active injury and GEMS adjusts your plan accordingly — reducing load, modifying workouts, and tracking recovery phases automatically.

🌙

Cycle Tracking

Optional menstrual cycle tracking for female athletes. GEMS adapts training intensity based on your cycle phase to optimize performance and recovery.

Strava Integration

Built around your Strava data

GEMS connects to the official Strava API to securely retrieve your activity data. Your Strava credentials are never stored in the app.

1

Connect your account

Tap "Connect with Strava" — you're redirected to Strava's official OAuth page to authorize GEMS. We never see your password.

2

Automatic activity sync

GEMS fetches your last 30 days of activities — runs, rides, and swims — and imports them with full metrics including laps and splits.

3

Smart performance analysis

Each activity is analyzed: VDOT for runs, FTP and W/kg for bike, CSS for swim. TSS is estimated and your zones are updated automatically.

Compatible with Strava

GEMS uses the official Strava API v3. We request read-only access to your activities — we never write, modify, or delete anything on your Strava account.

Privacy & Data

Your data stays yours

GEMS is GDPR-compliant. We collect only what's necessary to provide the service and never sell or share your data.

🔒

What we do with your Strava data

GEMS uses Strava activity data exclusively to analyze your athletic performance and provide personalized training recommendations. We request read-only access — we cannot post, modify, or delete anything on Strava on your behalf. Your Strava access token is stored securely in an encrypted database. You can revoke access at any time from the app or directly from your Strava settings. We do not sell, share, or monetize your Strava data or any personal information.

Data we collect

Email address for authentication, training profile (sport, level, goals), activity data synced from Strava, and optional health data (injuries, menstrual cycle) with your explicit consent.

Data we never collect

We never collect payment information, location data beyond what Strava provides, contacts, photos, or any data unrelated to your training.

Data retention

Your data is retained for as long as your account is active. Deleting your account removes all associated data within 30 days. No exceptions.

Infrastructure

Data is stored on Supabase (AWS eu-west-1, Europe). All communications are encrypted via TLS 1.2+. Strava tokens are encrypted at rest.
